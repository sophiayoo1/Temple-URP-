Reactivity of Pyrite and Acid Mine Drainage The iron sulfide, pyrite, is found at active and abandoned coal mining sites. Its decomposition in the environment leads to acid mine drainage (sulfuric acid generation) which is a significant problems for coal mining companies and the surrounding environment. The goal of the project is to look into strategies to suppress the oxidation of pyrite and the resulting acid generation. TU Main Campus Motivation
